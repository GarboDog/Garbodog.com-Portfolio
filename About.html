About Me

Hi! I'm GarboDog, a girl with too many ideas and projects in my head filled with funny. Dedicated towards creating creative content, exploring fun projects and learning new skills to bring more silly projects to light!

Primary focus is making comics, assets for websites, videos for Youtube, and stickers/YCHs for sale! Feel free to contact me via the formspree below or over on the Discord Server if youd like to chat about commissioning or working together on a project!!

Currently learning coding (C#, Javascript, UX Design, etc), game development (Godot and Unreal), animation (OpenToonz), and 3D modeling (blender).

I'm looking at collaborations with other streamers over on Twitch to help them make assets for their OBS/profile, doing concpet art for video games, video editing for other youtubers and varying other creative projects.
